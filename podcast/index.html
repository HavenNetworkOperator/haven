Episode 1 with Andrew Wilmot

"I built addictive tech. Here's what it's doing to our kids."

Andrew Wilmot spent years designing the very features engineered to keep us — and our children — glued to our screens. Now he's on the other side of it. We sit down to talk about what he saw from the inside, why it works so well on developing brains, and what parents can actually do about it.
