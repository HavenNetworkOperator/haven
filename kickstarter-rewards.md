# Haven — Kickstarter Reward Tiers

_5 tiers. All SIM-bearing tiers are pre-orders redeemable when Haven launches. Locked pricing is guaranteed for the life of the subscription. Adjust limits before publishing if you want more or fewer backers at each tier._

---

## Pricing Logic

Haven's planned retail price: **£12–15/month per SIM**
Founding member locked price: **£8/month per SIM**
Saving over 2 years: **~£96–168 per SIM**

The locked price isn't a discount code — it's a permanent rate for founding members. As long as you stay on Haven, you pay £8/month. Price increases don't apply to founding subscribers.

## Tier 1 — Haven Supporter

**Pledge:** £10
**Limit:** Unlimited

For people who believe in what we're building but aren't ready to switch SIMs yet. Your contribution goes directly into the platform build. You'll be recognised as a founding supporter in the app and in Haven's launch announcement.

**What you get:**
- Founding Supporter status — permanently listed in Haven's founding credits
- Name in the app (opt-in — you can stay anonymous)
- First access to Haven launch news before the public
- Warm feeling of having done something good

_No SIM included. This tier is pure mission support._

## Tier 2 — Haven Founding SIM

**Pledge:** £25
**Limit:** 500 backers

The core reward. One Haven SIM, at our founding member price, locked for life. When Haven launches, you activate your SIM and your £8/month rate begins — regardless of what Haven costs publicly at that point.

**What you get:**
- One Haven SIM (eSIM or physical — you choose at activation)
- Founding price locked: **£8/month** for the life of your subscription
- Founding member status — priority activation on launch day
- Founding member benefits: vote on the first 3 safety features we ship
- Direct channel to the founding team during beta
- Haven founding credits

_Estimated retail: £12–15/month. Founding members pay £8/month. Forever._
