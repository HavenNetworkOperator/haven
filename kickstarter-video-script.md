# Haven — Kickstarter Campaign Video Script

**Duration:** ~75 seconds
**Format:** Founder-to-camera + B-roll cutaways
**Tone:** Calm, direct, personal — no hype, no tech startup energy
**Location:** A real home environment. Kitchen table, living room. Not a studio.

_Scene directions are in [square brackets]. Replace [FOUNDER NAME] and [son/daughter/child] with your actual details._

## Script

---

**[0–10s — OPEN: B-roll, no voiceover]**

_[Cut to: close-up of a child's hands holding a phone. Screen slightly blurred. Then pull back — they're sitting at a kitchen table, someone else in the background. Ordinary. Familiar. Then cut to black.]_

_[Text on screen, white on black, in Fraunces serif:]_

> **"98% of UK mobile networks were designed for adults."**

---

**[10–25s — FOUNDER TO CAMERA]**

_[Cut to: founder, natural light, medium shot, at home. Not a desk. Not a studio. Direct to camera. No b-roll under this section — let the eye contact hold.]_

"I started Haven because my [son/daughter] is getting a phone soon. And when I looked at what was available — I couldn't find what I needed.

Every network treats a ten-year-old the same as a forty-year-old. And every parental control app? It's software. Sitting on top of a network that doesn't care how old your child is. Your child doesn't need to hack anything. They just need to find the passcode."

---

**[25–45s — THE PRODUCT: B-roll + VO]**

_[Founder continues VO over cutaway footage. B-roll: a SIM card being inserted into a phone; a clean, simple phone interface; a parent glancing at a weekly summary notification; a child using their phone naturally — messaging, not doom-scrolling.]_

"Haven is a mobile network — a SIM — built from the ground up for children aged nine to seventeen.

The safety is at the SIM layer. It can't be deleted, it can't be bypassed, it works on any phone.

Our AI detects grooming patterns and harmful content in real time — on-device, so your child's messages stay private.

And what you receive, as a parent, is a weekly summary — not a surveillance feed. You don't need to see everything. You need to know if something is wrong."

---

**[45–60s — WHY THIS CAMPAIGN: FOUNDER TO CAMERA]**

Founding member SIMs start at twenty-five pounds. That gets you one Haven SIM at eight pounds a month — locked for life, regardless of what Haven charges publicly.

We're targeting Q3 this year. Between now and then, every backer gets honest updates — not just the good news."

## Production Notes

**What to film:**
- Founder segment: natural light from a window, behind or to the side. Avoid overhead. Shoot at eye level — not slightly up or down.
- B-roll: a child's hands on a phone (not face — avoids consent issues and keeps it universal), a SIM card, a phone showing a simple notification, a parent at a kitchen table
- You don't need a camera crew. A modern iPhone on a tripod with a £30 Rode lav mic is enough for a Kickstarter video at this stage

**What NOT to do:**
- No upbeat background music under the founder segments — it undermines the direct tone
- No stock footage of children laughing at computers — it looks like every other edtech company
- No animations or explainer graphics — the script explains itself
- Don't try to show the actual Haven interface — it doesn't exist yet. Imply it with a notification on a real phone

**Subtitles:**
Add captions before uploading. Kickstarter plays videos on mute by default in the feed. Upload an SRT file or use Kickstarter's auto-captions and correct them manually.

**Thumbnail:**
Still frame or custom image: founder face (direct eye contact) with "The first mobile network designed for children" overlaid in Fraunces serif on a paper-toned background. High contrast. Readable at small sizes.

## Read-aloud timing check

_The script above times to approximately 75 seconds at a natural, unhurried pace. Read it aloud once before filming. If it feels rushed, cut the line "Our AI detects grooming patterns and harmful content in real time — on-device, so your child's messages stay private" and replace with a shorter version: "Our AI works on-device, so your child's messages stay private."_
