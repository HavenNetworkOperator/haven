For that first cold message. Short enough to read in a notification preview.

---

## Version 2 — Full Invitation (Email or DM follow-up)

Send this once they've said they're open to talking, or use it as a longer cold DM if their email is public.

---

**Subject:** Building Haven's founding council — would you join us?

Hi [Name],

I'm Rich Bowdler. I'm building Haven — Britain's first mobile network designed around family safety, not adults.

The short version: safety at the SIM layer, not an app, can't be deleted, works on any device. AI that detects grooming patterns in real time, on-device, with messages staying private. Parents get a weekly summary — not a surveillance feed.

Target launch: September 2026. Founding eSIM: £25 one-time, £8/mo locked for life.

---

**Why I'm reaching out to you specifically**

The Smartphone Free Childhood movement has done something remarkable — you've created hundreds of thousands of parents who delayed giving their child a phone and are now asking: *what does responsible look like when we finally do?*

Haven is that answer. But I'm not from SFC. I don't want to assume I know what that community needs.

I'm looking for 8 parents — people who are already in this conversation, who have strong opinions, and who aren't afraid to tell a founder when he's wrong — to be on Haven's founding council.

---

**What the founding council actually is**

This is not a corporate advisory board where I email you updates and ask for testimonials.

It's a monthly call, 45 minutes, where I show you what we're building and you tell me honestly whether it matches what parents actually want. You'd vote on which features ship first. You'd see things before anyone else.

Three things you'd get in return:

1. **Founding member status** — your eSIM is free, your £8/mo rate is locked for life, and you're listed on the site as part of the founding community
2. **First right to invest** — when we open our crowdfunding round (planned for late 2026), founding council members get first access and a 15% discount on shares
3. **Credit for what you build** — if Haven becomes what I think it can, this is the kind of thing you'd want your name on

Time commitment: one 45-minute call per month. Honest input. Nothing more unless you want it.

---

**What I'd ask of you right now**

Just a 20-minute conversation to see if this is a fit. No commitment, no pressure. If you think what I'm building is interesting but the council isn't right for you, that's fine — I'd still love your honest read on whether we're solving the right problem.

If you're interested, reply here and I'll send over a calendar link.

Rich Bowdler
Founder, Haven
[phone or email]
