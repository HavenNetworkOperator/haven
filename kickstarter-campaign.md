## The Problem

Every mobile network in the UK was designed for adults.

When you hand your child a SIM, you're putting them on the same network as everyone else — with the same defaults, the same exposure, and the same complete indifference to the fact that they're a child.

The parental control apps came later. Bolted on. Easy to delete. Impossible to keep current with threats that mutate daily. The moment your child knows the passcode to the App Store, the protection is gone.

And the threats aren't standing still. AI-powered grooming, deepfake blackmail, content engineered to be addictive — these aren't edge cases anymore. They're documented, they're growing, and no existing network is doing anything about them.

The Smartphone Free Childhood movement has mobilised tens of thousands of UK parents who delayed giving their child a phone. That was the right call. But delay has a limit. At some point, the phone happens — for safety, for independence, for the school WhatsApp group. When it does, the responsible option should exist.

It didn't. So we built it.

---

## Haven's Answer

Haven operates at the network layer — not as an app, not as a filter that sits on top of someone else's infrastructure. The protection is built into the SIM itself.

That means it cannot be deleted, bypassed, or turned off by your child. It travels with the SIM, not with the device. It works on any handset, any operating system, any browser.

The AI safety layer detects grooming patterns and age-inappropriate content in real time, on-device — so no conversation data leaves the phone. Your child gets privacy. You get peace of mind.

And when we surface something to you, it's a weekly insight — not a surveillance feed. You don't need to see every message. You need to know if something is wrong.

Haven is the phone plan that takes your child seriously as a person and takes their safety seriously as a design constraint. Not an afterthought. A starting point.

---

## How It Works

**Step 1 — The SIM does the work**
Haven SIMs run a custom safety layer at the network level. When your child uses data, voice, or SMS, the safety layer is active by default. No setup. No app to install. No settings to configure.

**Step 2 — AI that moves as fast as the threats**
Our on-device AI model monitors for grooming patterns, age-inappropriate content, and harmful contact in real time. It updates continuously — not quarterly like a filter list. When it detects something, it acts. When it's uncertain, it surfaces an insight to you.

**Step 3 — Summaries, not surveillance**
You receive a weekly insight summary — what kinds of content your child encountered, whether any patterns need attention, and what, if anything, we flagged. No transcripts. No message logs. Privacy for your child, clarity for you.

But the movement needs an alternative — not just a "no," but a responsible "yes." Haven is that alternative. The first mobile network that the SFC community can recommend. The first plan that treats giving your child a phone as something you can do without compromising on their safety.

We're launching now because the conversation is ready. We don't want to be five years late.
