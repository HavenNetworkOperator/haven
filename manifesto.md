# Haven Manifesto

---

**WE ARE PARENTS.**

Not investors. Not engineers who decided children were a market.

Parents who watched what happened when a smartphone arrived in a child's pocket with no guardrails, no signal, no off switch. Parents who said *enough* — and then did something about it.

We helped build the Smartphone Free Childhood movement. We signed the pacts, packed the school halls, held the awkward conversations with other parents. We know what it costs to hold the line. And we know what it costs when the line breaks.

Haven exists because no phone network was built with that line in mind.

---

**WE STAND WITH SCHOOLS GOING SMARTPHONE-FREE.**

When a school bans smartphones, it is making a statement: your education matters more than your notifications. Your friendships should happen face to face. You deserve to be present.

We believe that completely.

Haven will never undermine a school's smartphone policy. We will not sell a product designed to circumvent it. We are on the side of every head teacher who has taken a stand, every parent association that has pushed for change, and every child who is quietly relieved to have their phone taken away at the gate.

---

**SOCIAL MEDIA DOES NOT BELONG ON A CHILD'S PHONE.**

This is not a parenting opinion. This is Haven policy.

For any account registered to a child under 16, social media apps — Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat, X, and others — will not function on our network. Not by default. Not with a toggle. Not with a permission from a tired parent at 10pm.

We are not going to offer this as a feature. We are not going to make it optional. We are going to build it into the network itself, where no app update can undo it and no child can find the workaround.

We know the research. We know what the platforms know and have chosen not to act on. We are acting.

---

**SAFETY AT THE NETWORK LAYER.**

Every parental control that lives in an app can be deleted. Every filter that runs on a device can be bypassed with a factory reset or a friend's phone. We've watched parents install protection and watch it evaporate.

Haven's safety is in the SIM. It's in the network. It runs before the phone even knows a request has been made.

This is not a dashboard. It's not surveillance. It's infrastructure — the same way clean water is infrastructure. You don't monitor every drop. You build the system right and trust it.

---

**WE BUILT THIS FOR YOU.**

If you delayed giving your child a phone. If you signed a pact, had the conversation, held the boundary.

If you finally said yes — and then couldn't find a network that shared your values.

Haven is what we wish had existed.

It is a phone for childhood. Not a scaled-down adult smartphone. Not a feature phone pretending to be modern. A real phone, with real safety, built by people who understand exactly what's at stake — because we've been standing in the same playground, asking the same questions, as you.

---

**OUR PLEDGE.**

We will never knowingly weaken a protection to sell more plans.  
We will never add social media access for under-16s, regardless of demand.  
We will never pretend a dashboard is a substitute for real network-level safety.  
We will always be honest about what Haven can and cannot do.  
And we will always be on the side of the child.

*Haven. Built by parents. For the childhood that comes next.*
